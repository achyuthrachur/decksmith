SLIDE 5: Pillar 3 — Create Value
Transform How We Serve Clients
Clients don't buy AI. They buy lower risk, faster growth, reduced cost, and better decisions.
Step 1: Understand the Transformation
Host AI Workshops with clients
Map how AI will change their world — roles, processes, risks, opportunities
Answer: What do they need during transformation? What do they need after?
Step 2: Ask the Right Question
Instead of: "How can we use AI in this offering?"Ask: "Where in our client's value chain can AI materially improve outcomes?"
Outcome	What It Looks Like
Lower client risk	Continuous monitoring vs. periodic sampling
Faster client growth	Predictive recommendations vs. static analysis
Reduced client cost	30% lower delivery cost at the same price point
Better decisions	Insights delivered weekly, not quarterly
Competitive advantage	Analyze 100% of transactions — not just samples
Step 3: Do What Competitors Cannot Do at Scale
These are not incremental improvements — they are structural advantages
When we can do this consistently, value lift becomes revenue lift
Step 4: Evolve Our Offerings
Modify existing offerings to embed AI-driven outcomes
Create new offerings, platforms, and managed services where gaps exist
Ask: Can this become a subscription? A managed service? A performance-based engagement?
Step 5: Retool Our People
Shift from execution to judgment — fewer analysts doing manual work, more AI-augmented consultants
Develop new hybrid roles: AI-enabled solution architects, prompt engineers, automation designers
Invest in change management capability to lead clients through their own transitions
Step 6: Update Our Messaging
New market message: Crowe delivers AI-enabled outcomes, not just services
New internal message: Your expertise + AI = greater impact, faster
